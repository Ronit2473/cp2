ForecastPro

ForecastPro is an end-to-end retail analytics platform that combines sales forecasting, store performance monitoring, customer analytics, churn prediction, promo impact analysis, and an AI assistant to help businesses make smarter, data-driven decisions.

The project uses machine learning and time-series forecasting to estimate future sales, evaluates customer behavior through KPI dashboards, measures churn trends, and provides interactive visualizations and chatbot-based insights across multiple pages of the application.

What This Project Does

Sales Forecasting
Predicts future sales trends using time-series and machine learning features like lag values, rolling means, holidays, and promotions.
Store Performance
Tracks total sales, average daily sales, customer counts, assortment, store type performance, and other operational KPIs.
Customer & Churn Analysis
Measures customer behavior, churn rate, active vs churned users, and promo-related churn insights for better retention decisions.
AI Assistant
Lets users ask questions about KPIs, forecasts, churn, promotions, and uploaded data through a chatbot integrated into the platform.
